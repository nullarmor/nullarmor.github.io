Bashed - Hack The Box

Resolution of the Bashed machine

June 30, 2020 - 3 minute read -
HackTheBox

Bashed is an easy level machine based on Linux, the initial approach requires a basic enumeration to identify the webshell application. The privilege escalation process can be exploited in two ways: the first one exploits the kernel and spawn to us a root shell and the second one can be exploited by escalating the privileges through two Linux accounts: from www-data to scriptmanager and from scriptmanager to root.
